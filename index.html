Prom dress 2017: I designed and made this alongside my GCSE preparation aged 15. From when I started sewing it was always my goal to be able to design and make my own prom dress. When designing this dress, I was limitied by the width of the burgundy silk. This means, after many trig and circle calculations, I was able to make 7/8ths of a circle skirt. This lent itself very well to the weight and sheen of the silk as it sits smoothly over the hips and flows down with lots of folds
